A little story about me!

I am a muslim teenager living in South Tangerang, Indonesia. I live in the suburb where the growing population and the raise of many helpful people to bring prosperities in this community. When I was a little kid, I used to dream big, and I would think that one day I could become a character while gaining new experience. At the age of 9 something unexpected happened. My families and I we're giving a chance to study abroad at British Columbia, Canada for about two years. Overall, it was a once in a lifetime experience that I could never ever forgotten and my only wish for that is that I hope I can repay to those who had help me on educating me and supporting me while living as minorities. At 2012 I came back to Indonesia and continue my studies here, Fast forward I graduated from elementary school, and I continued to an Islamic junior high school, it was a lively school where I can say it was the most colorful experience I had. I continued my high school at an international Islamic boarding school, basically I was living a scholar life. Studying at an Islamic boarding school, makes me learn lots of knowledge very wide very vast very abundance. All at once I knew that the teachings that they had taught me will be useful for my future. After the graduation, I luckily had the opportunities to continue at a higher education at the university of Airlangga. And here I am …. still learning.

Achievement and experience

My biggest experience would be the two years of studying abroad. Because studying abroad and overcoming the struggle to survive it really opens your eyes of how you could view the world. As an Indonesian Muslim the district that I've been in to was very tolerant and there's many refugees so I'm not really the only one I have many friends from across the world. I've made two best friends; one is from South Korea his name is Daniel and second is Rahman from Iran. I didn't stop practicing my religion there instead while living as minorities I felt that my relation towards Allah grew more bigger in a way that I started to learn Arabic language and memorizing Al- Quran which by the way took a lot of time and I'm happy for it. For my biggest achievement is that I could be the representative of Indonesia on speaking about how coronavirus affecting the new waves of teaching towards student in an international audience, specifically on Southeast Asian country.
